Introduction

Sans the skeleton (also known as Sans the Skeleton) is a major character in the 2015 video game Undertale and is also featured in the 2018 sequel game Deltarune, where he can only be found at his shop which is a remodelled version of Grillby’s Diner from the original game.

Undertale

He is the older brother of Papyrus and first appears in Snowdin Forest after the protagonist exits the Ruins. Sans is the deuteragonist of the neutral and true pacifist routes, in which he keeps an eye on the protagonist throughout the game. He is also the final boss In the genocide route.

Deltarune

He is a recurring character in Deltarune and a returning character from undertale. Sans runs a self - titled grocery store in hometown as the “janitor” . Sans’s dialogues are written in lowercase and use the comic sans font, hence his name.
